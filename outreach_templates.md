# LeadVoixAI: Value-First Client Outreach Playbook

This playbook provides templates and step-by-step strategies for cold DMing real estate agents on LinkedIn, Instagram, and via email with zero budget.

---

## 1. The Psychology of Getting a Response

### Why Real Estate Agents Ignored Your Previous DMs:
* **The "I can help you" trap:** Sending a message like *"Hey, I have an agency that builds AI voice bots, let's hop on a call"* sounds like work to them. They receive 10 of these a day.
* **Lack of trust:** They don't know you, and they don't see immediate proof that your bot actually sounds good.
* **Vague pitch:** They don't understand *how* it helps them make money immediately.

### The Solution: Value-First Outreach (The "Shock & Awe" Method)
Instead of asking for their time, **give them a completed demo first**.
1. Look up their name or agency on Zillow or Instagram.
2. Find one of their active property listings.
3. Message them offering a **custom voice agent built specifically for that property listing**.
4. Send them to your website's **Live Browser Simulator** so they can hear the agent talk about real estate immediately.

## 3. How to Host Your Website for FREE (GitHub Pages or Vercel)

Since we want to launch with **zero budget**, you do not need to pay for web hosting. Here are the two best free hosting platforms:

### Option A: Hosting with GitHub Pages (Easiest)
1. Go to [github.com](https://github.com) and create a free account.
2. Create a new repository named `leadvoixai`.
3. Upload your `index.html`, `style.css`, and `app.js` files to this repository.
4. Go to the repository **Settings** tab.
5. In the left sidebar, click **Pages**.
6. Under "Build and deployment", set Source to **Deploy from a branch**. Select your main branch and `/root` folder, then click **Save**.
7. In 1–2 minutes, Google/GitHub will give you a free public link (e.g., `https://yourusername.github.io/leadvoixai`). You can use this link in your DMs!

### Option B: Hosting with Vercel (Fastest & Premium)
1. Go to [vercel.com](https://vercel.com) and sign up with your email or GitHub account.
2. Download the Vercel CLI or import your GitHub repository.
3. If importing directly, select the `leadvoixai` repository, leave the default settings, and click **Deploy**.
4. Vercel will give you a custom, premium domain like `leadvoixai.vercel.app` for free.
